‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I mix multiple sounds together?

1. Select your first sound from the library

2. Tap the "+" button to add more sounds

3. Adjust individual volume sliders for each sound

4. Save as a custom preset for future use (Premium feature)

How do I set a sleep timer?

1. Tap the timer icon on the main screen

2. Choose your desired duration (15 minutes to 8 hours)

3. Enable fade-out for a gentle volume decrease

4. The app will automatically stop when the timer ends

What's included in the Premium subscription?

Premium includes:

  • Access to all premium sleep sounds and themes
  • Create and save unlimited custom presets
  • Ad-free experience
  • Mix up to 5+ sounds simultaneously
  • Priority customer support

Can I use NapGenie offline?

Yes! Once sounds are downloaded, you can use NapGenie without an internet connection. Premium sounds are automatically downloaded when you subscribe.

Is NapGenie safe for babies and children?

Absolutely! NapGenie is designed for users of all ages. Our sounds are carefully curated to be safe and soothing for babies, children, and adults. Always use at comfortable volume levels.

üîß Troubleshooting

App Not Playing Sounds

  • Check your device volume and ensure it's not muted
  • Verify that Do Not Disturb mode isn't blocking audio
  • Close and restart the app
  • Restart your device if the issue persists

Subscription Issues

  • Ensure you're signed in with the same account used for purchase
  • Try the "Restore Purchases" option in app settings
  • Check your subscription status in your device's app store
  • Contact us if premium features aren't unlocking

App Crashes or Freezes

  • Update to the latest version of NapGenie
  • Restart your device
  • Clear app cache (Android) or reinstall the app
  • Ensure you have sufficient storage space

Timer Not Working

  • Check that your device isn't in power-saving mode
  • Ensure NapGenie has permission to run in the background
  • Verify the timer is set correctly before starting playback

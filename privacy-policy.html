Quick Summary: NapGenie respects your privacy. We collect minimal data necessary to provide our sleep sound services, use secure third-party services for subscriptions and analytics, and never sell your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create custom presets or use premium features
  • App Preferences: Your sound selections, volume settings, and timer preferences
  • Support Communications: When you contact us for help or feedback

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Analytics: App usage patterns, feature usage, and performance data (via Google Analytics)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Crash Reports: Technical data to help us fix bugs and improve the app
  • Advertising Identifier (iOS): With your permission, we may collect your device's advertising identifier to show personalized ads

1.3 Subscription Information

  • Purchase Data: Subscription status and purchase history (managed by RevenueCat and Google Play/App Store)
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by Google Play Store or Apple App Store (we never see your payment details)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core App Functionality

  • Provide sleep sound playback and mixing features
  • Save your custom presets and preferences
  • Manage your subscription status and premium features
  • Deliver personalized content and recommendations

2.2 App Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Fix bugs and enhance performance
  • Develop new sounds and features based on user preferences

2.3 Communication

  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send important app updates and notifications
  • Provide subscription and billing information

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to provide our app functionality:

  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and analytics
  • Google AdMob: Advertising for free users (with privacy controls)
  • Google Analytics: App usage analytics and performance monitoring
  • Google Play Services / Apple Services: App distribution and payment processing

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and users' safety.

3.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale, user information may be transferred as part of the business assets.

4. Data Security

  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on secure servers with access controls
  • Regular Updates: We regularly update our security measures
  • Limited Access: Only authorized personnel have access to user data

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

  • Permission Request: On iOS 14.5+, we ask for your permission before tracking you across apps and websites
  • Your Choice: You can allow or deny tracking - the app works fully either way
  • Personalized Ads: If you allow tracking, you'll see more relevant ads that help keep the app free
  • Change Anytime: You can change your tracking preference in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking

5.2 Access and Control

  • Data Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Data Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Data Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics and advertising (where applicable)

5.3 Subscription Management

  • Cancel subscriptions through Google Play Store or Apple App Store
  • Manage subscription preferences in your device settings
  • Contact us for subscription support

6. Children's Privacy

NapGenie is designed for users of all ages, including families with babies and children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. International Users

NapGenie is available globally. Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or our website. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated data retained for up to 2 years
  • Support Communications: Retained for up to 3 years
  • Deleted Accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@napgenie.com
  • Support: support@napgenie.com
  • Website: https://napgenie.com

Response Time: We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 48 hours.
